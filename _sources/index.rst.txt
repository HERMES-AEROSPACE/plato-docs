.. PLATO documentation master file

======================================================
PLATO — PLAsmas in Thermodynamic nOn-equilibrium
======================================================

**PLATO** is a high-fidelity library for the thermodynamics, transport, and
collisional-radiative kinetics of equilibrium and nonequilibrium multicomponent
gases and plasmas.

Written in Fortran 2003/2008 with C and C++ APIs, PLATO provides within a
single library a hierarchy of physicochemical models of increasing complexity:
from legacy multitemperature (Park-style) models, through coarse-grained
reduced-order models, to full collisional-radiative and state-to-state
descriptions.

.. note::

   Reference paper: Munafò & Panesi, *J. Thermophysics and Heat Transfer*,
   Vol. 39, No. 4, 2025. DOI: `10.2514/1.T7097 <https://doi.org/10.2514/1.T7097>`_
