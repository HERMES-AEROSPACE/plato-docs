Theory Guide

This guide presents the mathematical and physical foundations underlying PLATO. Each chapter derives the governing equations from first principles, states the assumptions, and points to the source files where the formulas are implemented. All notation is consistent across chapters; a summary is provided below.

The material follows the JTHT reference paper [MunafoPanesi2025] unless otherwise noted:

Munafò & Panesi, J. Thermophysics and Heat Transfer, Vol. 39, No. 4, 2025. DOI: 10.2514/1.T7097

The two PhD theses that underpin the theoretical framework are cited throughout:

  • Munafò thesis — State-to-state kinetics, coarse-grained models, and the unified multi-scale framework (Chapters 1–3).

  • Magin thesis — Two-temperature Chapman–Enskog transport theory, collision integrals, Stefan–Maxwell equations, Eucken correction, LTE thermodynamics including entropy and Gibbs free energy (Chapters 1–4).

Notation

All notation follows the JTHT paper. Source code variable names are noted in parentheses where helpful.

Symbol

Meaning (code variable)

\(k_{\mathrm{B}}\)

Boltzmann constant, \(1.380649 \times 10^{-23}\) J/K (UKB)

\(R_u\)

Universal gas constant, \(8.314462618\) J/(mol K) (URG)

\(N_A\)

Avogadro’s number (UNA)

\(h\)

Planck constant (UHP)

\(m_e\)

Electron mass (UME)

\(e\)

Elementary charge (UQE)

\(\varepsilon_0\)

Vacuum permittivity (UEPS0)

\(R_i = R_u / M_i\)

Species-specific gas constant (Ri)

\(M_i\)

Molar mass of species i, kg/mol (mm_comp)

\(m_i = M_i / N_A\)

Molecular mass of species i, kg (mass_comp)

\(n_i\)

Number density of species i, m-3

\(\rho_i\)

Partial mass density, kg/m3 (rhoi)

\(X_i\)

Mole fraction (Xc, Xi)

\(y_i\)

Mass fraction (yc, yi)

\(T_h\)

Heavy-particle translational temperature, K (Th)

\(T_e\)

Free-electron temperature, K (Te)

\(T_v\)

Vibrational temperature, K (Tv)

\(T_r\)

Rotational temperature, K (Tr)

\(p\)

Pressure, Pa

\(n_d\)

Total number density, m-3 (nd)

\(Q\)

Partition function (Q; denoted \(Z\) in the Munafò thesis)

\(\theta_{\mathrm{rot}}\)

Characteristic rotational temperature, K (theta_rot)

\(\theta_{\mathrm{vib},j}\)

Characteristic vibrational temperature of mode j, K (theta_vib)

\(\sigma\)

Molecular symmetry number (sym)

\(g_k\)

Degeneracy of electronic level k (g_el)

\(\varepsilon_k\)

Energy of electronic level k, in K (E_el; \(= E_k / k_{\mathrm{B}}\))

\(e_{f,i}\)

Formation energy at 0 K, J/kg (Ef_comp)

\(\bar{Q}^{(\ell,s)}_{ij}\)

Reduced collision integral (Q_11, Q_22)

\(A^*_{ij}\)

Collision integral ratio \(\bar{Q}^{(2,2)} / \bar{Q}^{(1,1)}\) (Astar)

\(D_{ij}\)

Binary diffusion coefficient, m2/s (Dij)

\(\Omega^{(\ell,s)}\)

Collision integral of order \((\ell, s)\)
